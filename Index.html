About NeuroPaws

NeuroPaws is dedicated to enhancing the bond between pets and their owners by providing insights into pets' emotional and mental well-being. Our wearable technology measures stress, cognitive function, and more to help you understand your pet's state of mind.

Key Features

Stress Monitoring

Track your pet's stress levels and get real-time insights into their emotional state.

Cognitive Insights

Analyze your pet's cognitive function and behavior patterns to keep them mentally healthy.

Bluetooth Connectivity

Sync data seamlessly to your mobile device and access insights anytime, anywhere.

Why Choose NeuroPaws?

  • Strengthen the bond with your pet by understanding their emotions.
  • Get valuable data to support veterinarians in providing better care.
  • Easy-to-use, wearable technology tailored for various pets.
